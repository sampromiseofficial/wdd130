WHITE WATER RAFTING

To provide unforgettable whitewater rafting experiences that connect people with nature, inspire adventure, and promote outdoor recreation in a safe and environmentally responsible way.

HISTORY

Our Whitewater Rafting was founded in 2020 with a simple but powerful vision: to help people reconnect with nature and faith through the raw exhilarating experience of whitewater rafting, our founders outdoor enthusiasts and community leaders set out to create more than just a rafting company.

Our early guests spoke of more than just adrenaline and scenery, they spoke of peace, renewal, and connection. Over the years, Psalm Whitewater Rafting has expanded its operations to include multiple river routes, seasonal tours, adventure packages for families, schools, and corporate teams.
